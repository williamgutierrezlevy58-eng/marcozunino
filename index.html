About Marco Zunino

Marco Zunino is a Peruvian actor and singer celebrated for his work in theatre and television. With a career spanning many years, he has earned recognition for his performances and is known for his presence across Latin American entertainment.

Renowned for his heartfelt performances and collaborations with international artists, Marco has built a devoted global fanbase. This official website is the place for fans to connect, get updates on new projects, tours, exclusive content, and special fan experiences.
